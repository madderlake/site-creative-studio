Objectives

Who are you trying to reach and what are you trying to accomplish? Precise details or broad concepts? Serious or entertaining? What’s your initial vision of the project?

Stakeholders

This should include everyone who gets to review and make decisions along the way.

Scope

For example, if this is an animation project, how many animations are you thinking about and what should the run time be for each one?

Last But Not Least

Deadline

We will need an estimated deadline so we can get started. This deadline needs to reflect when the project needs to be delivered to digital production.

Budget

Budgets can be tricky. Sometimes we can save a bunch, and sometimes to hit the right mark we’ll need a little more. Give us your ballpark to start.
