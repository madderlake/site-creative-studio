Instructor FAQs:

What's the difference between grading by Points vs Percentages?

In Grade Calculation by Points, the sum of the points earned are divided by the sum of the points possible to get the overall score. In Grade Calculation by Percent Average, the percentage score for all assignments are added together and then divided by the total number of assignments.

Why do some categories have weighting and others don't?

All categories you create display in the Progress App regardless of having a weighted value. Only categories that have a weighted value count towards the students grade. Uncategorized and Practice Assignment are never included in the students' weighted grade.

Can I export Attendance and Polling records?

Yes, you can export both Attendance and Polling records as a csv file and have it emailed directly to you.

Do Polling questions count towards my students' grade?

Polling questions only count if you export the results and import them via MindTap as an activity.

Why am I seeing "no active courses"?

You may have setup a learning product online but not yet created a course for it with a current start and end date. Make sure you create that course to go with your learning product and it should then appear in the app.

Do notes, highlights and flashcards I create on the web get synced with the app?

No. We are very sorry about this but annotations and study tools are specific to the mobile app and cannot be synced.

Student FAQs:

Why can't I login?

Make sure you are using your login credentials for Cengage. If you have credentials from your school's Learning Management System, they will not work. If you are a K-12 user, please see your administrator for assistance.

Why aren't my or my instructor's notes, highlights and bookmarks syncing with StudyHub?

Currently notes, highlights, and bookmarks taken in the mobile app are only saved and viewable on your phone. They will not get stored/saved on MindTap web in StudyHub, nor will notes, highlights, and bookmarks you or your instructor made on the web appear in the app. We are working towards supporting this functionality in the future.

Why can't I see my textbook?

Currently we support all MindTap textbook titles from 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019. Textbooks from 2014 and prior, as well as custom and international textbooks are not supported.

They will not get stored/saved on MindTap web in StudyHub, nor will notes, highlights, and bookmarks you or your instructor made on the web appear in the app. We are working towards supporting this functionality in the future.

Why can't I do my homework activities?

The app currently does not support doing homework activities. We are working towards supporting this functionality in the future.

Why isn't my audio playing for a video or flashcard?

Make sure your phone is not on silent mode.

What features of the app can I access from my Apple Watch?

Currently from your Apple Watch, you can view notifications, the rolling week view, activities list, and flashcards.

Do Polls count towards my grade?

Only if your instructor imports the results in MindTap.
